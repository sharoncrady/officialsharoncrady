About This Series

Audio Stories is my ongoing collection of original fiction, reflective monologues, and small cinematic scenes brought to life through my storytelling voice.

Each episode is designed to be easy to listen to in a single sitting—perfect for quiet evenings, late-night reflection, morning grounding, or a few minutes of escape between the noise of the day.

Over time, this series will grow to include stand-alone short stories, mini-series arcs, and themed collections.
