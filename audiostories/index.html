About This Series

Audio Stories is an ongoing collection of original short fiction, reflective monologues, and cinematic micro-scenes brought to life through my storytelling voice.

Each episode is designed to be easy to listen to in one sitting—perfect for quiet evenings, late-night reflection, morning grounding, or a small emotional reset during the day.

Over time, this series will expand to include stand-alone short stories, multi-episode arcs, and themed collections.
