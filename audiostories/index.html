About This Series

Audio Stories is an ongoing collection of original short stories, reflective monologues, and cinematic micro-scenes brought to life in my own voice.

Each story is designed to be easy to listen to in one sitting—perfect for quiet evenings, late-night reflection, a calming moment during the day, or an emotional reset.

Over time, this series will grow to include stand-alone short fiction, multi-episode mini arcs, and themed story collections.
