Narrative Support

Narrative Support is a behind-the-scenes service designed to help creators, developers, writers, and storytellers bring their ideas to life clearly and efficiently.

If you have a story in your head but don’t want to spend hours typing, formatting, or organizing it — I can help.

What I Help With

  • Typing and formatting story content
  • Transcribing spoken ideas into written narrative
  • Organizing story flow, dialogue, and scenes
  • Light polishing for clarity and readability
  • Supporting game narratives, apps, scripts, and creative projects

Who This Is For

  • Game developers
  • Indie creators
  • Writers with rough drafts or voice notes
  • Anyone who has ideas but hates typing

How It Works

You provide your raw material — notes, recordings, outlines, or rough text — and I turn it into clean, usable narrative content.

This service is collaborative, confidential, and tailored to your project.
