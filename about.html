About Sharon Fitzgerald

Solo parent • Hybrid artist • Future pilot • Multi-passionate entrepreneur

Hybrid Artist. Creative Triathlete. Builder of Worlds.

Sharon Fitzgerald is a creative force who blends resilience, imagination, and determination into everything she builds. A solo parent, creator, and future pilot, she is building a life that combines aviation, creativity, digital craftsmanship, and personal freedom.

Sharon is the founder of OfficialSharonCrady™, a growing multi-division creative ecosystem spanning writing, online publishing, digital products, voiceover, personal development, and web development. She believes in turning real-life challenges into opportunities — and she has done exactly that.

As a writer and creator, Sharon uses storytelling, journaling, and creative expression to inspire, heal, and awaken possibilities — both in herself and in others.

As a voiceover and voice acting student, she is building the skills to perform characters, narrate stories, and bring emotional depth to scripts of all kinds. Her voice work is rooted in authenticity, expression, and lived experience.

As a web developer, Sharon designs and builds her own websites from scratch — learning through doing, breaking, fixing, and refining. She actively develops digital spaces, creative tools, and online experiences that reflect her vision and growth.

As a future pilot, Sharon is pursuing aviation training and preparing to enter flight school, blending her love of freedom, travel, and precision into a long-term professional dream.

As a solo parent, she homeschools her son, teaches creativity and independence, and models what it looks like to build a life from passion, resilience, and vision.

Every corner of Sharon’s world — from her writing studio to her digital shop to her learning journey — is built around one belief:

Creativity can rebuild a life.
Expression can heal.
And freedom begins when we decide to design the life we truly want.

Here, you’ll find Sharon’s projects, writing, digital products, and works-in-progress — along with the things that inspire her along the way. This is where she builds, grows, dreams, and creates possibility.
