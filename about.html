About Sharon

A solo mom, creator, future pilot, and multi-passionate entrepreneur building a life that blends aviation, creativity, and freedom.

Hi, I’m Sharon — a creative writer, budding voiceover student, and entrepreneur behind OfficialSharonCrady.com. My journey has been one of resilience and creativity. I’ve learned to take challenges and turn them into opportunities for growth, healing, and expression. From homeschooling my son and exploring new creative outlets, to starting online businesses and building my skills in voiceover, I’ve discovered that every challenge carries the seeds of possibility.

Here you’ll find my projects, my shop, and the things that inspire me. I believe in the power of words, creativity, and self-expression to spark change and create possibilities — starting with our own.
